Neil deGrasse Tyson

Neil deGrasse Tyson was an astrophysicist, author, and science communicator, he is one of the most significant people in promoting space exploration and science education. He is most known for his theories on star formation, exploding stars, dwarf galaxies, and the structure of the Milky Way.

Accomplishments
Reclassified pluto as a dwarf planet.
Has a master degree in Astronomy.
Contributed in the Hayden Planetarium renovation
